Importance of Puja:
  1. Tradition & Blessings: Pujas honor tradition and seek blessings for the new home and its occupants.
  2. Spiritual Cleansing: Rituals cleanse the home of negative energies, fostering harmony and peace.
  3. Positive Energy: Mantras and prayers create positive vibes, promoting well-being and tranquility.
  4. Divine Connection: Pujas connect homeowners with divine forces, inviting blessings for a prosperous life.
  5. Psychological Comfort: Ritual participation eases transitions and fosters a sense of belonging.
  6. Community Unity: Involving family, friends, and neighbors strengthens social bonds and creates lasting memories.
